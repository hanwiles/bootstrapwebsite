This is Celestia, a VR game experience created for my Senior Final project. The development for this project spanned a two year period. It's the visual story of everything I learned these past 3 years, and where I hope to go with it.
This game I call FlyBoy and was the first ever real project I coded. I remember being so confused the whole time, but I just kept going with it, and I like to think I eventually learned something. Click the video to the left to watch a game trailer I made it for my GIMM 260 class.
This game, which we called Mirror, was a game I made second semester with a very talented group of coders and artists. I mainly focused on the artwork for this game, since we had very talented coders that know way more than me, but they helped us through their coding so we could learn.
